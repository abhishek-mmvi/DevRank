About IIITD Coders

Our Purpose

IIITD Coders is a platform designed to help students track their coding progress, foster a competitive environment, and provide professional networking opportunities. Our goal is to create a community where IIITD students can showcase their coding achievements and connect with like-minded peers.

How It Works

Students can create an account and link their Codeforces, LeetCode, and GitHub profiles. Our platform automatically tracks their progress on these platforms and updates their rankings on our leaderboard. Users can search for other students, view profiles, and connect with peers who share similar interests in competitive programming.

Getting Started

  1. Sign up for an account using your IIITD email address
  2. Link your Codeforces, LeetCode, and GitHub accounts
  3. Start solving problems on Codeforces and LeetCode
  4. Watch your progress and rankings update on our leaderboard
  5. Connect with other IIITD coders and grow together

Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or need support, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at support@iiitdcoders.com.
